About Us

Holland LLC© Corporate Consortium

Our Vision

We strive to bring the most innovative and accredited American “Medical Knowledge, Technologies and Clinical Research” advancements in Regenerative Medicine to physicians in other countries who are devoted to applying premium quality healthcare in adult stem cell regenerative medicine to help people suffering from inflammatory and/or degenerative chronic conditions to live healthier and longer.

Mission

To assist genuine investors, clinicians, and existing Healthcare Providers (Hospitals, Clinics, Medical Centers) in acquiring and making proper use of our Stem cell deployment technologies and skills, which we have developed since 2010, and to open their “American Center for Regenerative Medicine and Stem Cell Therapy” in their countries.

Goal

To establish a Network of state-of-the-art collaborative Stem Cell Clinical Therapy and Medical Research Institute at a number of worldwide locations. Our expertise will enable the quick development of cutting-edge science and technology at all global locations and will mentor the creation and success of the leading Centers for Regenerative Medicine worldwide.

1. The Purpose

To establish a highly credible and reliable multi-specialty “Research and Therapeutic Center” in “Regenerative Medicine Research and Applications and Stem Cell Treatment” for the benefit of the investor party, and operating with great responsibility and transparency under its auspices on a one-day-surgery system, under the Good Manufacturing Practice (GMP) form, the directives of the USFDA, and the relevant local authorities to help huge numbers of eligible patients (and noncritical cases) who are constantly being dispatched to receive treatment abroad despite its high cost and the hardship of travel. Giving the patients a new lease to enjoy a better life is what we strive for every day.

All our global medical centers apply the same internal quality and efficiency regulatory system and receive the same training, scientific and medical qualifications, clinical protocols, medical equipment, technical support, supervision, follow-up, and documentation methods. Teamwork in these centers helps us make the best medical decisions to achieve the best patient outcomes.

2. Our Company

“Holland, LLC®” Company (and its consortium of 8 companies) was formed and registered in San Diego, CA, USA, under the laws of the State of California, a designer and developer of “Regenerative Medicine” projects, with the vision to provide cellular therapy to people suffering from types of disorders, chronic, degenerative, and genetic diseases worldwide.

We are the leaders in designing and developing cutting-edge medical technologies based on stem cell research and their therapeutic biological properties, supported by leading biologists and biophysicists with a pioneering team in the area of stem cell biology, researchers, physicians, coaches, and technicians with the experience to deliver the technology to those in need.

We must confirm that we do not trade in medical devices, as some have suggested to us! We are a solid educational institution committed to providing science, first-hand knowledge, and training to clinicians, and promoting public awareness and brand loyalty.

“Holland, LLC®” Consortium (address in footnote) is keen to establish, develop, and support a state-of-the-art “Stem Cell Research and Therapy Center” specialized multidisciplinary, one-day-surgery facilities in "a Hospital, medical center, or a clinic" under the auspices of the investor.

Our primary focus is to design and develop novel treatment technologies for human diseases with state-of-the-art, stem cell-based therapies, and participate in setting the ethical rules. Stem Cell-Based Regenerative Medicine provides a new concept for treatments of diseases previously regarded as incurable. The company’s IP, Intellectual Assets, and medical technologies have been developed by our teams working on clinical and applied research and treatment projects at prestigious institutions over the past 18 years to promote and advance science, technology, and Good Practice of Regenerative Medicine applications.

3. Our Team

Our Consortium is a coalition of world-class scientists, physicians, scholars, researchers, and business individuals. The group integrates state-of-the-art human stem cell technologies with top-level biotechnology, physics, chemistry, clinical, and therapeutic expertise from academia and industry pioneers in this domain.

Our team members have been involved for the last 18 years in developing and writing stem cell-based scientific studies and clinical protocols for health disorders, chronic respiratory diseases, burns, and spinal cord injuries. Our adult stem cell technologies are well-developed; we have been using them for the last 18 years in our Network of 167 Medical Centers worldwide to treat many patients, which indicates the trust we enjoy among clinicians and patients equally.

Our team has well-documented, extensive multidisciplinary research and delivery experience in Stem Cell therapy (Mother Cells, or Primordial Cells) in association with major universities and elite research institutes, research and training cooperation to tune the scientific program to lay the groundwork for a new, safe, and effective cellular therapy. We put millions of dollars' worth of medical data and hard-won expert wisdom at everyone's disposal. Stem Cell Therapy: has the potential to repair, restore, replace, and regenerate cells, and could be used to treat many diseases.

Integrated Services

Holland LLC© Corporate Consortium jointly supplies the following integrative services, each by its professional specialty.

1.a. Research Studies and Clinical Trials

Conduct research and clinical trials in regenerative medicine by implementing approved cellular therapy procedures from the Medical and Science Committees. Monitor the trials and ensure that results are published in leading accredited scientific and medical journals.

1.b. Protocols

Developing surgical protocols for research and therapeutic cellular procedures, while ensuring compliance with U. S. regulatory licensing, including Institutional Review Board (IRB), FDA, and various prestigious academic references. Additionally, ensure the annual renewal of protocol licenses.

2. Training and Education

Offers a range of educational programs, hands-on trainings, and workshops tailored to partner programs. The specialized medical training focuses on regenerative medicine sciences, stem cell biology, and cellular therapy.

We empower physicians and medical staff with the vital skills they need to excel in regenerative medicine through our certified workshops and mentoring. Additionally, we are developing a comprehensive database that clearly defines Patient-Physician rights, obligations, and ethical standards to support the next generation of professionals.

Our accredited certification, endorsed by the Clinical Competency and Specialty Review Committee for Regenerative Medicine, meets U. S. IRB and FDA regulations. With over 12,000 trained professionals and nearly 4,000 courses in the past 12 years, we are dedicated to advancing regenerative medicine and improving global healthcare.

3. Medical Equipment Supply Chain

We are committed to enhancing quality control (Q/C) and quality assurance (Q/A) in the engineering and manufacturing procedures of medical and laboratory equipment and accessories for regenerative medicine and cellular therapy. Our automated solutions effectively address chronic diseases and support cell cryopreservation systems, all in compliance with U.S. FDA manufacturing regulations. Our approved equipment empowers healthcare professionals to improve patient care.

4. Project Structure Design

Design and implement regenerative medicine projects to benefit healthcare providers, adhering to local laws of the investor's country.

5. Education and Awareness Programs

Preparing accurate information for research and medical programs to promote community health education and awareness about regenerative medicine and stem cell treatments. This aims to enhance the role of specialized health centers in prioritizing patient welfare and to launch campaigns for health vigilance and public awareness, fostering a learning community to improve healthcare for all.

6. Conference Management

Set up and manage local and international conferences, forums, workshops, and symposia for regenerative medicine centers worldwide. These events provide vital opportunities for professionals to connect with leading medical scientists, speakers, and lecturers, and share research in prestigious scientific journals. Our international medical gatherings advance the field of regenerative medicine.

7. Contracting

A comprehensive management system that oversees legal, administrative, and financial matters, including the legal translation of international contracts and documents exchanged in international cooperation with project owners, regulatory authorities, and investing parties globally.
